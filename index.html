About Us

My name is Anas Ahmed and I have been dabbling in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript since 2008, but my true development journey began in 2020 during the pandemic. Faced with a personal project and unable to get help from a friend who was a professional developer, I decided to dive into coding myself. This experience ignited my passion for web development. Although I came from a chemical manufacturing family business and always thought I enjoyed it, I discovered a strong pull towards coding. It felt like coding gave me wings.

From 2020 to 2024, I dedicated myself to learning Node, Python, and PHP, creating several personal projects along the way, such as arcade games in Python, a project manager in PHP, and a General Knowledge challenge in Node, to name a few. The turning point came in early 2024 when a client from our family business asked me to build a website for them. It was then that I realized I couldn't live without coding. So, I made the bold decision to leave the family business and fully immerse myself in web development.

I am deeply passionate about coding and love creating modern, responsive web solutions. Whether it's developing complex applications or designing intuitive user interfaces, I am committed to delivering high-quality work that meets my clients' needs.
